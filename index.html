About Beauty Day To Day

  • Beauty Day to Day and Orange City Hair and Beauty Lab are led by Director Mr. Pradeep Markadkar.
  • He has been working in the beauty industry for the past 20 years.
  • He has organized over 500 seminars, workshops, and award events across Maharashtra and Chhattisgarh.
  • He promotes, markets, and brands various cosmetic brands and beauty professionals.
  • He is also actively involved in women empowerment initiatives.
  • He is working to develop a group of cosmetic companies and products.
  • He has designed a unique system keeping in mind the strong relationship between customer and company.
  • Beauty Day to Day is recognized as a trusted name in the salon industry.
  • He conducts workshops and seminars in various cities across India and abroad.
  • Aims to make Indian traditional and modern beauty salon products a valuable part of the global cosmetic market.
  • He manages the company professionally and specializes in the Indian cosmetic industry.
  • Constantly introduces new products while maintaining high quality standards.
  • Provides career guidance and complete planning for youth and new talent.
  • Over 100 youths have started their own businesses with his support and guidance.
  • Through Orange City Hair and Beauty Lab, he provides high-level professional training to new beauty professionals.
