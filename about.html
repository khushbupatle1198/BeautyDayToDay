About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2010, BeautyComp has grown from a small local competition to one of the most prestigious beauty event organizers in the country. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create a platform where beauty professionals could showcase their talent and creativity.

Over the years, we've expanded our events to include multiple categories, attracting participants and audiences from across the globe. Our competitions are known for their high standards, fair judging, and opportunities for winners to advance their careers.

Our Mission

Our mission is to elevate the beauty industry by recognizing and rewarding exceptional talent. We aim to:

  • Provide a fair and professional platform for competition
  • Promote innovation and creativity in beauty techniques
  • Connect talented professionals with industry opportunities
  • Educate and inspire the next generation of beauty experts
